Explore Lagos

Lagos is Nigeria's largest city. It lies in the South West of the country on the coast of the Gulf of Guinea. The city is bursting with a get-rich spirit that has made Nigeria’s economy the continent’s largest. It is known for its beach resorts, nightlife and activity.
